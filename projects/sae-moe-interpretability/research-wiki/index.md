# Research Wiki

This wiki stores persistent research memory for CodexResearchDesk.

Initial state:

- `papers/`: source paper notes
- `ideas/`: idea records and decision outcomes
- `experiments/`: experiment logs after a `GO` verdict
- `claims/`: claim and evidence mappings
- `graph/`: relationship edges

Do not treat wiki notes as sufficient authorization to run experiments. Use `tools/decision_gate.py`.
